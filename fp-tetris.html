Welcome to this game of Tetris, built entirely from HTML, CSS, and JavaScript! Use the left and right arrow keys to ove the tetrominoes, the up key to rotate them, and the down key to drop them faster. Complete rows to delete them and score points. The more rows you complete, the faster the game will go. Have fun! Credit for base game: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rAUn1Lom6dw
